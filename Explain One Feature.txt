Recruiter Task Management / Recruiter Panel :

One feature I worked on was related to recruiter task management, where APIs were developed to track recruiter tasks during the hiring workflow.

These APIs fetched lists of pending tasks such as candidate screening, offer making, and scheduling activities. (Screening, Scheduling, Offer Making, Offer On Hold, Joining)

The backend processed requests, retrieved task data from PostgreSQL using Hibernate, and returned structured responses to the recruiter dashboard.
